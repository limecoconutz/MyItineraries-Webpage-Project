Day 01 - 20 June


14:05 - 17:05 - flight Stockholm - Alesund.
Land at the airport and pick up the car rental from there. Check-in at hotelScandic Parken
Feel free to take some time to drive around the town. There's loads to see in the outskirts, also.
Evening walk and dinner in town. Alesund is a wonderful town, and since the sun doesn't really set up here this time of year, enjoy a late walk and view the Midnight Sun from the main Alesund Viewpoint.

Day 02 - 21 June


09:00 Drive from Alesund to Trollstigen (about 3 hours in total)
11:00 - 13:00 Take 2 hours for lunch at the Trollstigen cafe, and the short hike to see the views. (Remember to wear warm clothing.)
13:00 - Leave Trollstigen and drive to Linge to take the 14:00 Linge-Eidsdal ferry, then drive from Eidsdal to Geiranger


14:45 - Arrive in Geiranger and take some time to walk around and enjoy the views.
15:30 - Board the ferry from Geiranger to Hellesylt. Enjoy a rest and the amazing views from onboard the ferry, then drive from Hellesylt to Skei
19:35 - Arrive in Skei and check-in at Thon Hotel Jolster

Day 03 - 22 June


09:00 - Drive from Skei to Lavik to catch the 11:00 ferry to Oppedal, then drive from Oppedal to Bergen.
13:30 - Lunch in Bergen Brygge area, then walk around
15:00 - Check-in at Clarion Collection Hotel Havnekontoret
16:00 - Go up Floyen mountain with Floibanen funicular, then climb down on foot while enjoying the lovely scenery.

Day 04 - 23 June


09:00 - drive to Flam (about 3hours and 15 minutes). No ferries on this route.
11:30 - walkabout and lunch in town. Then take the hike up to Brekkefosen waterfall (about 2 hours in total)
16:00 - drive to Geilo (about 2 hours) and check-in at Geilo Mountain Lodge
Take a nice evening hike around the ski slopes, and enjoy dinner at the hotel.

Day 05 - 24 June


08:00 - drive to Oslo (about 3 hours and 15 minutes). No ferries on this route.
10:30 - Take a walk around the Oslo Opera House. Then, drive to Vigeland Sculpture Park have a walk and quick lunch at the cafe inside the park.
13:00 - Drive to the Oslo Central Station, turn i the car hire and board the train for Stockholm departing at 15:00.
