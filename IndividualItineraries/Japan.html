Japan Itinerary


12 cities in 20 days - your Japan trip challenge!

This is a once-in-a-lifetime trip! You will be discovering all the most important and interesting locations in Japan, tasting foods, and delighting in the local culture. This itinerary is packed-full of all the different experiences this culture has to offer, from tea ceremony, to samurai museum, to beautiful temple gardens and fabulous island resorts. Prepare your walking shoes: you'll need them!

Discover the day-by-day itinerary, created just for you:

Day 01 - Arrival, Shibuya district and cat cafe

After a long flight take some time to discover Tokyo! Have a walk through Shibuya district, and stop-over at a cat cafe.

Day 02 - Edo-Tokyo Museum and Asakusa area

Take the morning to explore the Edo Museum, and learn about the history of Tokyo city. It will take several hours to fully immerse into it, so give yourself the afternoon for fabulous Asakusa area.

Day 03 - Nagoya castle then Kyoto

Take the Shinkansen bullet train to Nagoya, and spend some time visiting the castle and the zen garden. Then, take the afternoon bullet train to Kyoto.

Day 04 - Arashiyama area with Bamboo Grove, Tenryuji temple and the Monkey Park

Have a walking tour of the Arashityama district. Have a hike up to the Monkey Park and hang out with these animals at the top of the mountain. Then, climb down, walk through the Bamboo Grove and relax at the Tenryuji Temple.

Day 05 - Tea Ceremony, Samurai Museum, the best ramen ever, and Kimono experience

This is a day full of experiences! Start it up with a traditional tea ceremony, than walk over and learn all about the Samurai at the Samurai Museum. Have a ramen lunch like not other. For the evening, dress up in a traditional kimono and walk through Higashiyama district.

Day 06 - Full day excursion to Nara

Take the morning train to Nara, and walk through the Deer Park. Take the entrance to Todai-ji Temple and wonder at the awesome buildings and the world's biggest Buddha statue.

Day 07 - Fushimi-Inari Hike, Kinaku-ji, Nanzen-ji and Philosopher's walk

Take the morning to do the Fushimi-Inari hike. Don't stop just half-way, go all the way to the top to enjoy the serene quiet. Then, visit Kikaku-ji and Nanzen-ji temples and finish the day with the famous Philosopher's walk.

Day 08 - Yamazaki whiskey distilleri and Royoan-ji zen garden

Have a morning train ride to the Yamazaki distilleri. There, learn everything about Japanese whiskey and enjoy a tasting exeprience. In the evening, vizit the Royoan-ji zen garden.

Day 09 - Osaka, Old Japanese Farmhouses and Dotonbori district

Take the morning Shinkansen bullet train to Osaka. Then, spend the day at the Open-air Museum, exploring the history of the Japanese farmhouse. In the evening enjoy a walk and dinner from street vendors in the famous Dotonbori district.

Day 10 - Osaka castle and Spa World

In the morning, take your time to visit Osaka temple. Then, it's time for rest&relaxation at the Spa World. This is a giant spa building with themed pools, saunas, and more.

Day 11 - Hiroshima, Peace Memorial Park

Take the Shinkansen bullet train to Hiroshima. Here, take your time to visit the Peace Memorial Park. Today, we overnight at a traditional Japanese house, with ricepaper walls and tatami bedding.

Day 12 - Miyajima and the floating Tori gate

Take the ferry to Miyajima. Here, take the funicular all the way to the mountain top and enjoy the fabulous views. Don't forget to have a walk around the floating Torii gate both in the morning and in the evening.

Day 13 - Fukuoka

Take the final Shinkansen trip of this tour all the way to Fukuoka. Here, have a relaxing day walking the city streets. Tomorrow, we go tropical!

Day 14 - Naha, Okinawa. A day in a tropical island.

Take the flight from Fukuoka to Naha, in Okinawa. Here, have a walk around town and enjoy the beach and the heat.

Day 15 - Ishigaki, a small island in Okinawa

Take the flight from Naha to Ishigaki. Here, you can take a tour of the fabulous tropical beaches, do some exploring of the nearby caves or just relax at your spa resort.

Day 16 - Hokkaido, Sapporo, and Mount Moiwa hike

Take a flight from Ishigaki to Sapporo. Here, have a hike up Mount Moiwa and enjoy the spectacular views. Take an evening walk around town.

Day 17 - Sapporo Beer Museum, Otaru

Start the day with a visit to the Beer Museum. Then, take the train to Otaru. The trip will take you just on the edge of the Ocean, so make sure to keep your camera handy!

Day 18 - Otaru sightseeing

Spend the day walking through the quaint town of Otaru. Make sure to stop by the main shopping street and taste some local specialties. Then, take the train back to Sapporo and the flight to Tokyo.

Day 19 - Final day in Tokyo - make the best of it!

We are approaching the end of our trip. Take the day to enjoy Tokyo, get all the experience you still need, and make sure you go home with a heart full of memories.
